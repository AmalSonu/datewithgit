Welcome to ACADEMICS - V, where excellence meets education! Our institution is committed to nurturing young minds, fostering creativity, and empowering students to reach their full potential. Here's what sets us apart:
  • Our Vision

At ACADEMICS - V, we envision a world where knowledge knows no bounds. Our mission is to create a vibrant learning ecosystem that inspires intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and holistic development.
1. College of Arts and Sciences

Our liberal arts college offers a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs. From literature to mathematics, students explore their passions and develop a well-rounded foundation.

2. College of Engineering and Technology

Cutting-edge labs, expert faculty, and industry partnerships define our engineering programs. Whether it's robotics, software development, or sustainable energy, we prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

3. College of Medicine and Nursing

From nursing to public health, we train compassionate healthcare professionals. Our state-of-the-art simulation labs simulate real-world scenarios, allowing students to hone their skills.
1.NSP Scholarship

NSP is a web based application that provides single stop solution for end- to-end scholarship process right from submission of student application, verification, sanction and disbursal to end beneficiary for different types scholarships provided by the various Ministries/Department of Government of India.
